MediGraph AI
a clinical knowledge graph that thinks with you

MediGraph AI unifies patients, encounters, conditions, medications, providers and observations into one connected graph — then layers decision support, population health, clinical NLP, grounded GraphRAG Q&A and FHIR interoperability on top, for real clinics and hospitals in the US and India.

Why a graph?

Clinical reality is a network, but most systems store it in disconnected tables.

The problem

A patient's story is spread across admissions, labs, prescriptions and providers. Relational silos make "which of my AF patients aren't anticoagulated?" or "who is overdue for an HbA1c?" slow, brittle questions — and the answers rarely come with their evidence.

The MediGraph approach

Model the record as a property graph, attach a curated clinical knowledge base (SNOMED / ICD-10 / RxNorm / LOINC), and compute transparent, citable decision support over it. Every answer can point back to the exact nodes and guidelines it came from.

Architecture

Pluggable at every layer — swap the data source, the graph store, or the LLM without touching clinical logic.

What's inside

Eight capabilities, one shared core. Everything below runs in the live demo.

🧑‍⚕️

Patient 360 & decision support

LACE readmission, CHA₂DS₂-VASc, HAS-BLED, eGFR/CKD staging, care-gap detection and drug-interaction screening — all transparent and citable.

📈

Population health

Disease prevalence, HEDIS-style quality measures, utilisation and risk stratification across the whole panel.

👥

Cohort builder

Compose conditions, medications, demographics and lab thresholds into registries for outreach and reporting.

🕸️

Knowledge graph

Live subgraph exploration plus provider-network PageRank centrality and community detection.

📝

Clinical NLP

Dictionary NER with negation detection, SNOMED/RxNorm/LOINC mapping, and guideline linking from notes.

💬

GraphRAG Q&A

Natural-language questions answered from graph facts with citations — and a read-only guardrail on any generated query.

🔌

Interoperability

FHIR R4, HL7 v2 and C-CDA adapters with vendor profiles for 35+ US & India systems.

🛡️

Security & compliance

Role-based access, JWT auth, append-only audit trail and HIPAA Safe-Harbor de-identification.

Run it in two minutes

No database, no API key — the offline demo is the default.

git clone https://github.com/AravindB98/medigraph-ai.git
cd medigraph-ai
pip install -r requirements.txt

# Clinical UI  →  http://localhost:8501
streamlit run ui/streamlit_app.py

# REST + FHIR API  →  http://localhost:8000/docs
uvicorn medigraph.api.main:app --reload

Sign in with a demo role (clinician / nurse / analyst / admin / auditor). Add a .env later to connect live Neo4j, Snowflake or OpenAI — each independently.

⚕️ Research & demonstration software. Not a certified medical device; not FDA/CDSCO cleared. All bundled data is synthetic. Risk scores and AI answers are decision-support aids, not a substitute for clinical judgement.
